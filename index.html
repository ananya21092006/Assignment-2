About Us

Welcome to Moon Walk Salon

Moon Walk Salon is a premier unisex salon located in the heart of Gurugram. We are dedicated to providing exceptional hair and beauty services in a relaxing and welcoming environment.

Our team of skilled professionals are passionate about their craft and committed to helping you look and feel your best. We use only premium products from trusted brands like Lakme and Tresemme to ensure the highest quality results.

Whether you're looking for a fresh haircut, a glamorous makeover, or just some well-deserved self-care, we have the expertise and experience to exceed your expectations.
